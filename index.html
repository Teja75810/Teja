Classic Upma Recipe

Ingredients for Classic Upma:

  1. 2 tbsp oil
  2. ¾ tsp mustard
  3. 1 tsp cumin
  4. 1 tsp urad dal
  5. 1 tsp chana dal
  6. 1 dried red chilli
  7. Few curry leaves
  8. 1 inch ginger, finely chopped
  9. 2 chillies, finely chopped
  10. ½ tsp salt
  11. 3 cups water
  12. 1 tsp sugar
  13. 1 cup rava / semolina, coarse
  14. 1 tsp ghee
  15. ½ lemon juice
  16. 2 tbsp coriander, finely chopped

How to Make Classic Upma:

Firstly, in a large kadai heat 2 tbsp oil. Splutter ¾ tsp mustard, 1 tsp cumin, 1 tsp urad dal, 1 tsp chana dal, 1 dried red chilli, and a few curry leaves. Add ½ onion, 1 inch ginger, 2 chillies, and ½ tsp salt. Sauté until the onions soften slightly. Further, add 3 cups water and 1 tsp sugar. Get the water to a rolling boil. Now, keep stirring with one hand and add 1 cup rava slowly. Make sure to keep stirring to prevent lump formation. Cover and simmer for 2 minutes or until the rava absorbs all the water. Add 1 tsp ghee, ½ lemon juice, and 2 tbsp coriander. Mix well. Finally, enjoy North Karnataka style Uppittu with coconut chutney.
